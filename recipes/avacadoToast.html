Avacado Toast

Home Page

This avacado egg toast will leave you feeling full yet wanting more because it's just so delicious!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Avacados
  • Lemon
  • Salt
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Eggs
  • Black pepper

Recipe

  1. In a non stick skillet, melt 1-2 teaspoons of butter over low heat. In a bowl, crack an egg and whisk it well. Add some salt and pepper to taste. Pour in the whisked eggs into the skillet.
  2. As it sets, move the egg with your spatula from side to side to form large, soft curds. Cook until full cooked, but not dry.
  3. Top your avocado toast with the egg and sprinkle some pepper flakes, spices or topping of your choice.
